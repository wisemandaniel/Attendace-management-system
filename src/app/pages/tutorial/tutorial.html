Welcome to AAT

The Automatic Attendance tracker app is a mobile application that enables students or employees to automatically take their attendance once they get into the classroom or job sites.

How to use the app

Create an account. Make sure to fill all the information correctly. Next, login to your account and view all your attendance history. When you are connected to the school network, or your office network, your attendance will be automatically taken.

Register your courses.

Why must I register my courses? You need to register a course before your attendance for that course can be tracked.
